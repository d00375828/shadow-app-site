# Shadow App — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** November 13, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Shadow (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Shadow mobile application (the “App”).

## What we collect

- **Account info (optional):** Name, email, and profile photo if you choose to add them in the App.
- **Audio recordings (when you press record):** Practice pitch recordings you create for AI feedback.
- **App usage data (limited):** Basic diagnostics (e.g., crashes, performance) to improve reliability.
- **Support messages:** If you contact us, we may keep your message and email to respond.

We **do not** sell your data. We **do not** track you across other companies’ apps or websites.

## How we use data

- Provide AI-generated feedback on your recordings.
- Improve features, quality, and stability.
- Communicate with you about updates or support requests.

## Where data is processed

Recordings may be processed by our cloud services to generate feedback. These services follow industry standards to protect data in transit and at rest.

## How long we keep data

- **Recordings:** Kept until you delete them in the App or request deletion.
- **Account & support info:** Kept as long as needed to operate the service and comply with law.

## Your choices

- **Microphone & Photos:** The App will ask for permission before accessing your microphone or photos.
- **Delete:** You can delete recordings in the App. You can also request data deletion via the Support page.

## Children’s privacy

Shadow is not directed to children under 13. If we learn we collected personal information from a child under 13, we will delete it.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We’ll update the “Last updated” date above when we do.
