# Shadow App — Support

Need help with Shadow? We’re here.

## Contact

- **Email:** andy@shadowsales.com

## Common topics

- **Account & profile:** Update your name, email, and profile photo in Settings → Profile.
- **Recordings:** If a recording fails to upload or process, try again on stable Wi‑Fi. If issues persist, email us.
- **Permissions:** Microphone and Photos access are required for recording and profile images. You can change permissions in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security.
- **Data requests:** To request deletion of recordings or your account data, email us from your account email address.

## Testers (TestFlight)

When reporting issues, please include:

- Your iOS version and device model
- The App version/build number (from Settings → About inside the App, if available)
- Steps to reproduce and any screenshots or screen recordings
